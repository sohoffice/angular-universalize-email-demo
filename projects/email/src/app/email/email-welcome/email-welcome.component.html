This is a welcome letter powered by angular-universalize-email.

angular-universalize-email is an open source utility that allows an angular project to generate email templates using angular universal.

You can develop your email template with Angular, and add them to any server side technology.

You may also pair it with cliend side template engine to produce the email body.
However, this is not recommended.
