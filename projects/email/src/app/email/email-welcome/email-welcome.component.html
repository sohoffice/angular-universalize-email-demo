This is a welcome letter powered by angular-universalize-email.

angular-universalize-email is an open source utility that process angular pages into email templates with angular universal plus other conversions.

You can develop your email template with Angular, test it with ng serve. Process it when you're happy and add the result to your server side stack.

You may also choose to pair it with client side template engine to produce the full email body and feed the mail provider directly.
However, this may create relay and spam issue, so is not recommended.

Hope you enjoy it.
